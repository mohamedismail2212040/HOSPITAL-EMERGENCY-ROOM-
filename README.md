# Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI analytics solution designed to monitor, analyze, and optimize emergency room (ER) operations.
It provides actionable insights into patient flow, wait times, satisfaction levels, referrals, and demographics to support data-driven decision-making for hospital administrators and stakeholders.

This project transforms raw ER data into interactive dashboards that help identify bottlenecks, peak periods, and areas requiring operational improvement.

---

## Business Objective

To enhance operational efficiency and patient care quality by:

* Tracking key ER performance indicators (KPIs)
* Identifying trends, patterns, and peak workload periods
* Supporting strategic planning and resource optimization
* Improving patient wait times and satisfaction levels

---

## KPIs Covered

* Total Number of Patients
* Average Wait Time
* Patient Satisfaction Score
* Number of Patients Referred
* Admission vs Non-Admission Rate
* Patients Seen Within 30 Minutes
* Patient Demographics (Age, Gender, Race)
* Department Referral Analysis
* Hourly and Daily Patient Volume

---

## Dashboards Included

### Dashboard 1: Monthly View

**Objective:**
Monitor ER performance on a month-by-month basis to identify short-term trends and variations.

**Key Insights:**

* Patient admission vs non-admission status
* Age group distribution (10-year intervals)
* Gender and race demographics
* Department referral patterns
* Percentage of patients seen within 30 minutes
* Patient volume by day and hour

---

### Dashboard 2: Consolidated View

**Objective:**
Provide a holistic summary of hospital ER performance over a custom date range.

**Key Insights:**

* Aggregated KPIs across multiple months
* Long-term trends in wait time and satisfaction
* Overall patient load and referral distribution
* Peak days and hours across the selected period

---

### Dashboard 3: Patient Details

**Objective:**
Enable granular, patient-level analysis for detailed investigation and troubleshooting.

**Fields Included:**

* Patient ID
* Patient Name
* Gender
* Age
* Admission Date
* Patient Race
* Wait Time
* Department Referral
* Admission Status

---

### Dashboard 4: Key Takeaways

**Objective:**
Summarize insights from all dashboards into clear and actionable conclusions.

**Highlights:**

* ER handled 9,216 unique patients over 19 months
* Average wait time of approximately 35.3 minutes
* Average patient satisfaction score of approximately 4.99
* Nearly 50% admission rate
* Peak load observed on Saturdays, Thursdays, and Sundays
* High referral volume to General Practice and Orthopedics
* Balanced gender distribution and diverse patient demographics
